-------Mathletes Club........





Mathletes is an opportunity for students to explore and compete in areas of math topics determined by grade level. Students compete with other schools. Mathletes have a continuous goal to remain an award winning club. Procedure For Joining Students should have a strong interest in math and be academically eligible to join. Information regarding meeting times and locations will be read during the daily announcements.
